Who Am I?

My name is Ankush Agrawal. I am a business minded, technically focused student at UC San Diego. I am driven by the thrill of creation, stories of success, and an insatiable desire to make the world a better place. One step at a time, I am gaining the skills I need to make it happen.

Skills

iOS Developer

Scripting

I love writing little (or not so little scripts). Some would call the tasks I don't want to do a result of laziness. I see these as opportunities to practice development and better utilize my time. I try to write scripts for everything to free up more time in the long run

Business
